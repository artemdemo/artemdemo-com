Hi everyone,

My name is Artem, and I'm a frontend developer based in Tel Aviv. I decided to start this blog for a few reasons:

First, I truly believe in knowledge sharing. It’s what drives the web industry forward, and every developer benefits—both those who share and those who discover helpful information online.

Second, writing helps me solidify my own understanding. We often don’t fully grasp a concept until we try to explain it to someone else. Although it might feel counterintuitive, putting ideas into words is one of the best ways to deepen comprehension.

If you’re interested in my open-source projects or code examples, feel free to check out my Github account.
